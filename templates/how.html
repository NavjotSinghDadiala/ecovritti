How We Work

End-to-end smart waste management workflow

Smart Waste Management Workflow

🏠

Household Segregation & QR Scan

Each household segregates wet & dry waste. Waste is linked to the household through QR code scanning.

🚛

Collector Visit with Smart Bin

Collector carries a portable smart bin. If segregation is wrong → alert triggered & corrected. If right → points rewarded.

🗑️

Area-Level Bin & Monitoring

Waste is transferred to area-level smart bins with IoT sensors. Fill levels tracked in real time, alerts sent to municipality.

📱

App/Website Tracking & Rewards

Citizens track compliance reports & rewards. Municipality dashboard shows real-time data & segregation trends.
